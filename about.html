Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our services and operations.

What is microfinance?

Microfinance is a type of banking service provided to low-income individuals or groups who traditionally lack access to conventional banking and related services. It includes small loans, savings accounts, and other financial products designed to help people start or expand small businesses and improve their financial situation.

How do I apply for a loan?

You can apply for a loan through our online portal or by visiting one of our branches. The process involves filling out an application form, providing necessary documentation, and meeting with our loan officers for assessment. We offer various loan products tailored to different needs and circumstances.

What are the interest rates?

Our interest rates vary depending on the loan type, amount, and term. We strive to keep our rates competitive and affordable while ensuring the sustainability of our operations. All rates are clearly disclosed upfront, and we provide detailed loan calculations so you know exactly what you'll pay.

Do I need collateral?

Most of our loan products do not require traditional collateral. Instead, we use alternative methods such as group guarantees, character references, and cash flow analysis to assess loan applications. This makes our services accessible to those who lack substantial assets.

How long does approval take?

Loan approval times vary depending on the loan type and complexity of the application. Simple loans can be approved within 24-48 hours, while more complex applications may take 3-5 business days. We work efficiently to process applications while ensuring thorough assessment.

What happens if I can't make a payment?

If you're experiencing financial difficulties, please contact us immediately. We understand that circumstances can change, and we're committed to working with you to find a solution. This might include payment restructuring, temporary payment holidays, or other arrangements based on your situation.

First 1,000 Clients

2020

Reached our first milestone of serving 1,000 clients, expanding our loan portfolio and introducing group lending models to serve more communities effectively.

Digital Transformation

Launched our digital platform and mobile application, making it easier for clients to access services, make payments, and track their loan status remotely.

Obtained our full license from the Bank of Zambia, enabling us to expand our service offerings and serve a broader range of clients across the country.
